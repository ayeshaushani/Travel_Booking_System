Day 1: Arrival in Colombo

Colombo → Negombo (30km)
12:00 PM

Airport Pickup

Meet your guide at Bandaranaike International Airport and transfer to your hotel in Negombo.

2:00 PM

Check-in & Lunch

Check in at the beachfront hotel and enjoy a welcome lunch featuring Sri Lankan cuisine.

4:00 PM

Negombo City Tour

Visit the fish market, Dutch canal, and St. Mary's Church. Sunset at Negombo beach.

Day 2: Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Negombo → Sigiriya (180km)
7:00 AM

Morning Departure

Travel to Sigiriya with stops at a spice garden and local village.

1:00 PM

Climb Sigiriya Rock

Ascend the 5th-century rock fortress with its famous frescoes and lion's paw entrance.

6:00 PM

Sunset at Pidurangala

Hike to Pidurangala Rock for panoramic views of Sigiriya at sunset.

Day 7: Return to Colombo & Departure

Galle → Colombo (120km)
9:00 AM

Coastal Train Journey

Scenic train ride along the coast from Galle to Colombo (optional).

2:00 PM

Colombo City Tour

Visit Gangaramaya Temple, Independence Square, and local markets.

6:00 PM

Airport Transfer

Transfer to the airport for your departure flight (3 hours before flight time).
